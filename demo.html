HOW THE SCORE WORKS
HUMAN algo v3 — five weighted inputs
View Velocity
Likes + shares per hour in the first 60 min after post. A video hitting 80K likes/hour on a $NEAR mention scores higher than 2M total likes on a week-old post.
35%
Cross-Creator Corroboration
Same coin mentioned by 3+ unrelated creators within a 2-hour window. Single influencer ≠ signal. When @cryptowhale88, @altcoin_alice, and @defi_detective all post $NEAR independently — that's signal.
30%
Creator Track Record
Each creator has a historical accuracy rating. Calculated from 90 days of their past coin calls vs actual price action within 24h. Mega-influencers often score low — they post after the pump.
20%
Comment Buy Intent
NLP on top 200 comments. "Where do I buy this" vs "already sold" signals very different momentum. We weight buy-intent comments against cope/exit comments.
10%
On-Chain Cross-Check
DEX volume spike, wallet inflows on L1/L2 for the coin. If on-chain already moved, the TikTok signal is late — score penalized. If on-chain is flat, it's early — score boosted.
5%
Score 0–49 = noise / 50–69 = watch / 70–84 = signal / 85–100 = strong signal. We do not issue "buy" or "sell" — only signal strength.
